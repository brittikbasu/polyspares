About

Polyspares is an India based exporter of textile machine spare parts and other textile products. We focus on high precision engineering, quality and durability.

We have over 30 years of experience working in the textile Industry. This helps us understand your requirement better. We can give you options on what could be the best product as per your needs. If need arises, we are willing to travel to your location and solve any problems faced by you. Because of all the attention and inquiries we're getting from Brazil, Egypt, Indonesia, Turkey, Iran and Colombia, we would like to establish ourselves as a top global exporter of textile machine spare parts.

Our Products

Spare Parts

We supply good quality spare parts of Texturing machines as well as Spinning Machines. When our customers try our samples they are impressed and even more confident in doing business with us. Be it Murata, Barmag, Himson or Alidhra, We've got you covered.

Ceramic Components

Our ceramic components' inventory includes Intermingling jets, guide disk and all types of yarn guides. We've got good feedback on our intermingling jets from our clients in Bangladesh, so be sure to give our samples a test run.

Antistatic Coning Oil

Our Antistatic Coning oil is well suited for high speed texturising and acts as a superior lubricant on high speed knitting and weaving applications. It reduces frictional filament damages. We're super confident on the reliability of our Antistatic Coning oil because of the base material used, which is of exceptional quality.

Processing Components

When it comes to processing components, we've got everything you may need. Silicon spray, Stainless Steel Sand (S.S. Sand),Gaskets and Spin Pack Filters. All these can be custom made for your specific requirement.
